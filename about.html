💡 Why Informatics?


Informatics is an interdisciplinary program that blends technology, data, and human-centered design. While computer science focuses heavily on technical skills and programming, informatics teaches us to put people first. It's about using technology thoughtfully to solve real-world problems and create meaningful change.


What makes informatics special is its focus on bridging the gap between data, technology, and people. To me, it's is about the “why” behind the technology. We don't just write code - we design solutions that are user-centered and impactful.
